What does CloudBookings do?

CloudBookings is a comprehensive platform that allows you to search, compare, and book flights from multiple airlines, both domestic and international. We make it easier for you to find the best deals and travel options all in one place.

Do I need to create an account to book a flight?

You don't need an account to search for flights, but creating an account allows you to save your preferences, view your booking history, and receive special offers and notifications. It's a convenient way to manage your bookings and streamline the process for future trips.

Are there hidden fees when booking through CloudBookings?

No, CloudBookings does not charge any extra fees. The price you see when you are redirected to the airline's or provider's website is the final price. Any fees related to seat selection or additional services are determined by the airline or provider.

Can I book international flights on CloudBookings?

Absolutely! CloudBookings offers both domestic and international flight options, allowing you to explore various destinations around the world.

How can I contact CloudBookings if I need help?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can reach out to our customer support team via email at support@cloudbookings.com.
